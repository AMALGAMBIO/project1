Vision
NovaGames

Delivering games that will always remain in the hearts of viewers

What we consider most important are the fans who look forward to our works.
Those fans who cry, laugh, and have their hearts touched by the stories we bring to life...
Thinking of each and every face on the other side of the screen, we wish to create games that makes them say to themselves, “I am glad I encountered this game”, that makes them want to play it
over and over again.
As a studio, we strive to deliver games that will remain in the hearts and minds of our fans, while challenging new forms of expression and experimenting with various genres without focusing on
any particular style.
